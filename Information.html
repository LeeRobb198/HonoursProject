How to use Flight Detector

Search For Flights

The application allows you to search for flights by clicking the search button then entering the four following parameters:
  1. Country of origin
  2. Minimum altitude
  3. Maximum altitude
  4. If the plane is on the ground
Once you have entered the parameters, you can click the Search button at the top of the search tab. All the flights meeting your search criteria will display on the map.

Track a Flight in AR

The application allows you to select a flight from the map and track it in AR. You must first choose a flight that is within the outer circle of your location then click the AR button.

Once you have clicked the AR button, the browser will ask you to allow Flight Detector to use your location. You should see the icon as you rotate on the spot.

Don't worry if you can't see it! Just make sure to allow the application to access your motion and orientation. You can do that by clicking on the screen, once the camera visual has loaded, and a popup will display asking you for permission.
